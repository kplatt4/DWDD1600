What is Toothpillow?

Toothpillow is a online myofunctional therapy platform to help kids with jaw development, by wearing our appliance while sleeping at night and for 1-2 hours a day, our product will help childrens jaws develop correctly, which helps solve a number of problems. wearing the appliance will help you spend more time in non-REM, or "deep" sleep helping your child feel well rested during the day. Leading to better behavior during the day.

Why Toothpillow?
