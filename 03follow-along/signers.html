Who were the signers?

Famous signers

Thomas Jefferson
Thomas Jefferson, a delagate from Virginia, was the principal author of the Declaration of Independace. He later served as the 3rd president of the united states and is remembered for his contrubutions to American ideals of liberty and democracy.
John Adams
John Adams, representing Massachutses, was a vocal advocate for the independance of the Continental Congress. He became the 2nd president of the United States and played a key role in drafting foundational documents and promoting the cause of freedom
